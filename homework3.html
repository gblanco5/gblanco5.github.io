Making learning fun

ABC Toys is a company that opened its doors in 2001 and it has kept its mission for all those years.

The mission of our company is to create toys that are safe for kindergarten kids but that challenge the kids' mind and motor skills making learning a fun task.

By the way, parents love our toys too

Our Toys

Gummy Candy Lab

Learn about the scientific properties of natural polymers as you mold your own delicious gummy candies using carrageenan, a natural gelatin-like ingredient called that comes from seaweed!

Drill Activity Center

Children love using the Drill to create their very own designs or following the included patterns to make specific pictures. Just snap any one of the drill bits into the power drill or screwdriver, grab a handful of big bolts and power up the creative fun!

Submarine Playset

Who knows what creepy creatures you'll see when you explore the ocean's bottom with the Deep Sea Submarine Playset from Animal Planet.

Math Set

This two-book set with included Hot Dots pen provides children with 100 lessons on a variety of math skills appropriate for preschool, from counting numbers and identifying shapes to math facts and measurement.

Spelling Board

Colorful wooden letters in their proper places to spell words on the cutout 8 inch two-sided wooden boards. Sixteen playful pictures with three and four letter words keep learning fun!
